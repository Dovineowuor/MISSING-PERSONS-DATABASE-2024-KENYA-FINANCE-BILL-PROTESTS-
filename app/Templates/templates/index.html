How It Works

Welcome to the Missing Persons 2024 Antifinance Bill Demonstrations Kenya database. This AI-powered platform is designed to help track and identify individuals who have gone missing during the #RejectFinanceBill2024 protests. Our mission is to assist in reuniting families with their loved ones by providing a centralized and accessible online resource for all Kenyans.

Search for Missing Persons

Use our advanced search tools to locate individuals reported missing during the protests. Enter details to find information quickly and accurately.

View Detailed Information

Access comprehensive profiles and updates on missing persons. Our database provides essential details to aid in the identification and reunification process.

Report and Connect

Report new cases or connect with other users to share information. Our platform enables community support and collaboration in locating missing individuals.
